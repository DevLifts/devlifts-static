Taking a breather

Over the past 3 years, we've helped thousands of developers from around the world refactor their bodies. We are grateful to have had the opportunity to see so many meet their goals — and in some cases, far and above exceed them. We've accomplished everything so far with a true MVP — spreadsheets, Zapier automations, MailChimp automations, et al. Now that we've amassed 3 years of experience helping developers get fit, we've decided to take an indefinite hiatus to take what we've learned and make an even better platform for helping developers get fit in the future.

We aren't sure when we'll be back, as we both have other things to attend to. But we'll be sure to let you know.

In the meantime, if you are still looking for some remote personal training, you can reach out to Thad directly at thad[at]devlifts[dot]io.

Keep crushing it!
— Thad & J.C.
