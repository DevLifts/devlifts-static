The Why

Why DevLifts exists

We exist because I know what it’s like to be a developer and start accepting aches & pains, depression, anxiety, bad sleep, weight gain, and a host of other health problems as reality. It wasn’t until I started changing my lifestyle that I discovered these problems didn’t have to be my reality. We exist because I want other developers to come to the same realization. We exist because we want to help them learn how to cultivate a healthy lifestyle.

J.C. Hiatt

Founder at Devlifts

Join the community

Join the DevLifts community and take control of your health.

Upon signing up, we’ll schedule a video call and get to know you, your goals, how you feel, your experience level with exercise and dieting, and more. We’ll then create a customized training plan tailored to your individual needs and check in with you each week to answer your questions and make sure you’re on track to achieve your goals.

You’ll also be invited to join our private Slack community, where you can ask us questions directly and share your progress and what you’re learning with other developers who are also on their fitness journey.

Our first 100 customers will receive

  • A 30 minute video call with the founders, Thad (a certified personal trainer) & J.C., so we can learn about you and your goals
  • An individualized, 8 week training & nutrition plan, tailored to your goals and preferences
  • Access to our private DevLifts Slack community
  • Regular Check-Ins to make sure you're on track
